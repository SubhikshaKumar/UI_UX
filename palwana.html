DISCOVER PALAWAN

Palawan is the Philippine's answer to paradise. This island province stretches southwest to Borneo with lush limestone peaks rising from a jewel-like sea so clear, that you can almost see the expressions on the fish from above the surface. Slivers of gleaming white sand fringed with rustling palms rim many of these jungle-clad islands, while under the water, coral reefs flourish with an impressive diversity of tropical fish, offering some of the best diving in the world. Other attractions include the islands' unique wildlife, emerald lakes, and quaint fishing villages. Coron is home to plush resorts, and El Nido drips with natural beauty and is one of the most alluring islands in the chain. From here, you can island hop around the spectacular Bacuit archipelago. One of Palawan's top attractions is the World Heritage-listed Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, an impressive limestone cave system with a long underground river.

WATER SPORTS

SUBWING

Subwing is a new revolutionary watersport that reinvents the way we explore the sea. Subwing makes human underwater flying possible, with virtually full freedom of movement, letting you glide trough the water like a dolphin. Subwing Palawan brings fresh and exciting experience of underwater flights right in to Port Barton. With 3 different tour packages, anyone can find something for themselves. Our most popular ride takes an hour, but if you think that it won't be enough for you, book all day long Subwing tour and enjoy more exclusive experience like secret spots and delicous lunch served on beautiful island. Breathtaking views and amazing wildlife will leave you excited for a long time and make your trip hard to forget. All Subwing tours can be easily booked on our website and paid with card or PayPal. You can also visit our booking office if you are already in Port Barton. Subwing Palawan takes part in marine conservation program to spread the awareness of the marine ecosystem and coral reefs protection.
Swimming: Not Required
Tips: Keep your eyes wide open and feel the thrill.
Approximate Cost: 60 – 90 USD per person to fly for an hour

BALI SURFING & WHITE WATER RIFTING

Adrenaline junkies will thrill to the ultimate Bali combo: the ride of your life on a giant swing, followed by a white-water rafting trip down the scenic Ayung River. Capture photos as you soar above the jungle, then whoop with joy as you paddle the rapids. Tour includes lunch, door-to-door round-trip transportation, and a tea and coffee tasting. Feel the adrenaline rush as you soar high above the jungle on a giant swing Thrill as you race the Ayung River’s rapids on a raft Savor lunch and enjoy a coffee and tea tasting Relax with hotel pickup and drop-off—and a small group of maximum 15
Best time to revel in this sport: April to October
Swimming: Preferred
Tips: For two hours ocean Kayaks are offered free of charge. Do enquire about this from your instructor.
Approximate Cost: 30-50 USD per person

JET SKIING

What is spending the day on the beach without trying to ride on a jet ski? Jet skiing is one of the most famous activities in Palawan and anywhere else where there is water! Jet skiing has become one of the most popular water sports simply because of the adrenaline rush that it causes to anyone who decides to ride a jet ski. The waters of Palawan adds to the pleasure and confidence that it brings to one’s adventurous soul taking you to a different level of sensation.
Swimming: Not Required
Tips: If you already know how to dive, do tell your instructor about it. He will help you get an ultimate experience. Whatsoever, do listen to your instructor carefully.
Approximate Cost: 130 – 1000 USD per person depending upon the diving course selected by you.
